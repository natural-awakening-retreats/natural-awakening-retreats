Ajah Mendonça

“I moved to Portugal over 5 years ago, looking to connect further with my Portuguese roots. During my time here, I have adapted to the challenges that come with moving abroad and am proud to now call Lisboa ”Minha Casa”. With over a decade of experience, I find great joy in helping others connect to their own bodily awareness through massage therapy. My interests extend to social connection, music (you can likely find me on a dance floor somewhere in the city), and overall well-being. I find importance in living a balanced life, while avoiding societal pressure to fit into one box.
After all, we are human beings: all diverse and multifaceted."
